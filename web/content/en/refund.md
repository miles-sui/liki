# Refund Policy

This policy explains our approach to refunds for 25 Types.

## All Features Are Free

All features on 25 Types are free of charge. There are no paid subscriptions, one-time purchases, or activation codes required to access any feature — including personality assessments, Bond analysis, Flow forecasts, peer reviews, historical tracking, and data export.

## Donations

Donations are voluntary contributions made to support the service. A donation does not purchase any features, rights, or privileges.

**Donations are non-refundable.** They represent a gift of support, not a commercial transaction. If you believe a donation was made in error (for example, an incorrect amount or unauthorized use of your payment method), please contact us and we will review your case.

## How to Reach Us

Contact us with your account username and details of your request. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
