Direct Answer

The process for choosing a good name: first, determine your Xi Yong Shen from BaZi → select radicals matching the needed Five Elements → ensure reasonable stroke counts → finally, aesthetics and meaning. Most people reverse this—prioritize sound and meaning, then check Elements. That is backward.

Key Signals · BaZi

  • Step 1: Determine Xi Yong Shen Five Element. This is the foundation.
  • Step 2: Select corresponding Element radicals. Wood favors wood-radical characters, Fire favors sun/fire radicals, and so on.
  • Step 3: Check stroke counts. At minimum, 2/3 of Heaven, Person, Earth grids should be auspicious.
  • Step 4: Good meaning and sound are bonuses, not core.

Decision Map

  • Follow the signals above. Structural clarity replaces guesswork.

Why You're Asking

You are not asking out of superstition—you are asking to understand your structure so you can move forward with clarity.
