Direct Answer

The complete naming process: cast the BaZi chart → determine Day Master strength → identify Xi Yong Shen → select radicals by Element → compose the full name → check stroke counts → final confirmation. Every step matters.

Key Signals · BaZi

  • Day Master strong → Xi Yong Shen leans toward draining/controlling (Cai, Guan, Shi Shen/Shang Guan).
  • Day Master weak → Xi Yong Shen leans toward supporting (Yin, Bi Jie).
  • Five Element choice is based on overall BaZi balance—not filling what the Day Master lacks, but what the chart needs.
