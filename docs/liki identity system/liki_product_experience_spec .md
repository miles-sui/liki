Liki Product Experience Specification v1.0

Product North Star

English

Understanding before Choice.

中文

先理解，再选择。

---

1. Product Philosophy

English

A naming product should help people understand before they choose.

Its purpose is to support thoughtful naming through understanding, exploration, and reflection.

The product provides guidance and insight, while the final choice always belongs to the user.

中文

一个优秀的起名产品，应帮助用户在做出选择之前，先获得充分理解。

它通过理解、探索与思考，帮助用户完成更有意义的命名过程。

产品提供分析与建议，而最终的选择始终属于用户。

---

2. Experience Philosophy

English

Every interaction should leave users with greater clarity than before.

The experience should feel calm, transparent, respectful, collaborative, and confidence-building.

The product should reduce uncertainty through understanding rather than authority.

中文

每一次交互，都应让用户比开始时拥有更多理解与更清晰的判断。

整体体验应体现平静、透明、尊重、协作，并帮助用户建立信心。

产品应通过帮助用户理解来减少不确定性，而不是依赖权威感。

4. Decision Principles

English

The product should support human decision-making rather than replace it.

When multiple reasonable options exist, it should encourage comparison instead of presenting a single "correct" answer.

Recommendations should always preserve user choice.

中文

产品应支持用户做决定，而不是替用户做决定。

当存在多个合理方案时，应鼓励用户进行比较，而不是提供唯一的“正确答案”。

任何建议都应保留用户自主选择的空间。

---

5. Interaction Philosophy

English

Interactions should feel collaborative rather than authoritative.

The product should seek understanding before offering guidance, provide context before conclusions, and respect the user's intentions throughout the experience.

中文

产品交互应体现协作，而不是权威。

在提供建议之前，应先理解用户需求；在给出结论之前，应先提供背景与依据；整个过程中始终尊重用户的真实意图。

---

7. Human Agency Principle

English

The product exists to augment human judgment, not replace it.

Technology provides analysis and guidance.

People retain ownership of every meaningful decision.

中文

产品的目标是增强人的判断，而不是取代人的判断。

技术提供分析与建议。

每一个重要决定，都始终由用户自己掌握。
