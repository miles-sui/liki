Liki Design Language Specification v1.2

1. Design Philosophy

English

Morning light over still water.

Liki's design is inspired by the quiet balance between light and water.

It represents clarity, balance, and thoughtful discovery.

It is calm rather than dramatic, timeless rather than trendy, and illuminating rather than overwhelming.

Every visual element should express clarity, harmony, and thoughtful discovery.

中文

晨光映照静水。

灵机的设计灵感源于光与水之间宁静而平衡的关系。

它象征着清晰、平衡与发现。

整体视觉追求平静而非张扬，经典而非流行，启发而非震撼。

每一个视觉元素，都应传达清晰、平衡与发现的体验。

这是智者原型的视觉表达——设计不为吸引注意，而为启发理解。

«Internal Design Philosophy（内部设计哲学）

丙火照壬水 — The Sage illuminates deep water. 智者照见深水。」

13. Experience Principles

The design experience follows the Brand Experience Principles. Every visual and interactive element should support them.

English

- Guided, not instructed — Visual hierarchy leads the eye. Nothing competes for attention.
- Inspired, not overwhelmed — One focal point per view. Whitespace is content, not emptiness.
- Confident, not anxious — Consistent patterns build trust. No visual surprises.
- Culturally enriched, not superstitious — Traditional materials in modern forms. Never mystical.

Users should leave with greater clarity rather than greater uncertainty.

中文

设计体验遵从品牌体验原则。每一个视觉与交互元素应为其服务。

- 被引导，而非被命令 — 视觉层级引导视线。没有争夺与喧嚣。
- 被启发，而非被灌输 — 每个视图一个焦点。留白是内容，不是空白。
- 更有信心，而非制造焦虑 — 一致的模式建立信任。没有视觉意外。
- 文化滋养，而非渲染神秘 — 传统材质，现代形态。不涉玄学。

用户离开时，应获得更多清晰，而非更多疑惑。
