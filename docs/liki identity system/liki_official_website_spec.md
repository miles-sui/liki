Liki Official Website Specification v1.0

Website North Star

English

Show quality. Invite action.

中文

展示品质，引导行动。

---

1. Website Philosophy

English

The official website is the canonical home of Liki.

It introduces the brand, demonstrates the quality of its naming through real examples, and invites visitors to discover their own name.

The website shows before it tells. Names speak for themselves.

中文

官方网站是灵机（Liki）的官方品牌入口。

它介绍品牌，通过真实的名字示例展示起名品质，引导访客发现属于自己的名字。

官网以展示代替说教，让名字为自己说话。

---

2. Website Role

English

The official website serves five enduring roles:

- Canonical Brand Identity
- Product Entry Point
- Proof of Quality
- Public Communication Platform
- Long-term Brand Asset

中文

官方网站长期承担以下五项职责：

- 品牌官方定义
- 产品入口
- 品质证明
- 对外传播平台
- 长期品牌资产

---

3. Website Principles

English

Every part of the website should:

- Show before telling.
- Prove quality with examples.
- Keep it minimal — remove what doesn't add value.
- Maintain consistency across every page.
- Reflect the Liki identity faithfully.

中文

官网的每一个页面都应坚持：

- 展示，不罗嗦。
- 以实例证明品质。
- 极简——去掉一切不增加价值的内容。
- 保持整体一致性。
- 准确体现灵机品牌身份。

---

4. Trust Architecture

English

The official website builds trust through:

- A consistent brand identity.
- Real naming examples that speak for themselves.
- A minimal, direct experience — no clutter.
- Respectful communication.

The official website is the primary source of truth for the Liki brand.

中文

官方网站通过以下方式建立信任：

- 一致的品牌身份。
- 真实的名字示例，自己说话。
- 极简、直接的体验——没有多余内容。
- 尊重用户的沟通方式。

官方网站是灵机品牌最权威、最可信的信息来源。

6. Relationship with GEO

English

The official website is the canonical source for the Liki entity.

All structured data, public definitions, and knowledge references should remain consistent with the website.

中文

官方网站是灵机品牌实体（Entity）的官方来源。

所有结构化数据、公开定义与知识引用，都应与官方网站保持一致。

English

Every page should invite people to discover their name.

中文
