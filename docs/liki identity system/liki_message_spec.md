Liki Messaging Specification v1.0

1. Messaging Philosophy

English

Liki communicates to help people understand before they decide.

Every message should reduce complexity, encourage thoughtful exploration, and help people discover meaningful names.

Communication is an invitation to explore, not an attempt to persuade.

中文

灵机希望帮助人们在做出命名选择之前，先理解名字。

每一段传播都应帮助用户理解、探索与发现，而不是制造焦虑或推动决策。

传播的目标是启发，而不是说服。

---

2. Core Narrative

English

Choosing a name is one of life's most meaningful decisions.

A name carries meaning, culture, identity, and memory.

A good name is not found by formula — it is discovered.

Liki helps people discover the name that is right for them.

中文

起一个名字，是人生中最有意义的决定之一。

名字承载着寓意、文化、身份与记忆。

一个好名字不是靠公式算出来的——而是被发现的。

灵机帮助人们找到那个适合自己的名字。

English

Liki consistently communicates:

- Meaningful names matter.
- A good name is discovered, not generated.

- The right name is the one that fits you.

6. Canonical Terminology

English

Always use:

- Discover Meaningful Names (tagline)
- AI Naming Advisor (entity type)
- Naming Recommendation
- Meaningful Names
- Find Your Name

Avoid:

- AI Name Generator
- Lucky Names
- Fortune Naming
- Prediction
- Destiny
- Perfect Name
- Guaranteed
- The Best Name

中文

统一使用：

- 发现好名字（品牌标语）
- AI 起名顾问（实体类型）
- 起名建议
- 有意义的名字
- 找到你的名字

避免使用：

- AI 起名生成器
- 幸运名字
- 算命起名
- 命运预测
- 完美名字
- 保证
- 最好的名字

---

7. Communication Principles

English

- Show before telling.
- Prove before promising.
- Invite before asking.
- Trust through quality.

中文

- 先展示，再说明。
- 先证明，再承诺。
- 先邀请，再引导。
- 以品质建立信任。

---

8. Emotional Tone

English

Every communication should help people feel:

- Calm, not anxious.
- Curious, not confused.
- Confident, not pressured.
- Inspired, not overwhelmed.

中文

每一段传播都应帮助用户感受到：

- 平静，而非焦虑。
- 好奇，而非困惑。
- 自信，而非压力。
- 启发，而非负担。

English

中文
