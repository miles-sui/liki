2. Brand Archetype

English

The Sage.

Liki embodies the Sage archetype — driven by the pursuit of understanding and the belief that knowledge leads to better decisions.

The Sage seeks understanding through inquiry, values wisdom over convenience, and shares knowledge to empower others.

For Liki, this means: help people understand what makes a name meaningful, rather than simply give them a name.

This archetype naturally explains why Liki is knowledgeable, thoughtful, trustworthy, and calm — these are the Sage's native traits, not arbitrary choices.

中文

智者。

灵机体现智者原型——以追求理解和"知识导向更好决策"为驱动力。

智者通过探索寻求理解，重视智慧甚于便利，分享知识以赋能他人。

对灵机而言，这意味着：帮助人们理解什么让一个名字有意义，而非简单地给他们一个名字。

这一原型自然地解释了为什么灵机专业、理性、可信、平静——这些是智者的原生特质，而非随意选择。

5. Brand Positioning

Target Audience

English

Liki serves people who believe names matter. They value cultural depth and thoughtful analysis over quick answers. They want to understand their options, not just receive a result.

- Primary: Parents naming a child, seeking names with cultural meaning and personal significance.
- Secondary: Adults exploring a new Chinese name or understanding their current one.
- Broader: Anyone curious about the language, culture, and tradition behind Chinese names.

中文

灵机服务于那些相信名字有意义的人。他们重视文化深度和理性分析，而非快速答案。他们想要理解自己的选项，而非仅仅得到一个结果。

- 主要：为孩子起名的父母，希望名字兼具文化内涵和个人意义。
- 次要：探索新中文名字或理解现名的成年人。
- 更广：对中文名字背后的语言、文化、传统感到好奇的任何人。

Competitive Differentiation

English

- Unlike fortune-telling services, Liki analyzes — it does not predict.
- Unlike name generators, Liki balances multiple dimensions — it does not randomize.
- Unlike traditional naming masters, Liki is accessible, transparent, and AI-augmented — not esoteric or authoritative.

Liki occupies a unique space: the intersection of AI, linguistics, cultural tradition, and human judgment.

中文

- 与算命服务不同，灵机分析而非预测。
- 与起名生成器不同，灵机多维度平衡而非随机组合。
- 与传统命名师傅不同，灵机开放、透明、AI 增强——而非玄奥或权威。

灵机占据一个独特的位置：AI、语言学、文化传统与人的判断力的交汇。

6. Brand Beliefs

English

We believe:

1. A meaningful name emerges from balance, not from a single rule.

3. Tradition is a source of wisdom, not a set of fixed prescriptions.
4. AI should illuminate options, not obscure them.

我们相信：

2. 语言承载文化。一个好名字兼顾音韵与寓意。
3. 传统是智慧的源泉，而非僵化的教条。
4. AI 应当启发选择，而非遮蔽选择。
5. 最好的名字，是人在理解与信心中做出的选择。

English

中文

| 特质 | 含义 |
|---|---|
| 专业 | 根植于语言学、文化与传统——而非主观意见。 |
| 理性 | 多维度考量后提供观点，而非草率结论。 |
| 可信 | 每个建议都有可审视的依据。 |
| 平静 | 给予思考空间，从不催促决策。 |

Voice

English

| Situation | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| Recommending a name | "This name flows well — 'Ming' evokes brightness and clarity." | "This name is auspicious and will bring good fortune." |
| Presenting options | "Here are three names, each with different strengths." | "This is the best name." |
| Explaining methodology | "We consider sound, meaning, cultural context, and traditional principles." | "Our AI calculates the perfect name." |
| On uncertainty | "Several names could work well. Here's what each offers." | "I guarantee this name suits you." |
| Cultural references | "This character appears in Tang poetry, where it conveys…" | "This character has mystical properties." |

中文

| 场景 | 应该 | 不应该 |
|---|---|---|
| 推荐名字 | "这个名字音韵流畅，'明'字有光明之意。" | "这个名字五行补火，一定大吉大利。" |
| 呈现选项 | "三个名字各有特点，你可以比较看看。" | "这绝对是最好的名字。" |
| 解释方法 | "我们从音韵、字义、文化背景和传统原则来综合考虑。" | "我们的 AI 算法计算出最完美的名字。" |
| 面对不确定性 | "几个名字都不错，各有侧重。" | "我保证这个名字最适合你。" |
| 文化引用 | "这个字出自唐诗，表达的是…" | "这个字有神奇的力量。" |

The line is not about terminology — it is about prediction. Describing a name's elemental profile is analysis. Claiming it will bring fortune is prediction. Traditional knowledge should illuminate understanding, not assert certainty.

界限不在于术语，而在于预测。描述名字的五行特征是分析。声称名字能带来好运是预测。传统知识用于启发理解，而非确定性断言。

8. Brand Story

English

A name is one of the first gifts a person receives.

It carries language, culture, meaning, and identity.

For centuries, understanding what makes a name meaningful required a lifetime of learning.

Today, that knowledge is within reach.

Liki helps everyone discover names that are thoughtful, meaningful, and enduring.

中文

名字，是人生收到的第一份礼物。

它承载语言、文化、寓意，也承载身份认同。

千百年来，真正理解一个名字的意义，需要穷尽一生学习。

今天，这些知识触手可及。

灵机帮助每一个人发现兼具意义、文化与生命力的名字。

9. Brand Experience Principles

English

Every interaction with Liki should leave people feeling:

- Guided, not instructed — We illuminate paths. We don't issue directions.
- Inspired, not overwhelmed — One insight at a time. Depth over volume.
- Confident, not anxious — Clarity reduces uncertainty. We explain before we recommend.
- Culturally enriched, not superstitious — Tradition as wisdom, not mysticism.

中文

每一次使用灵机，用户应当感受到：

- 被引导，而非被命令 — 照亮道路，而非发号施令。
- 被启发，而非被灌输 — 一次一个洞察。深度而非信息轰炸。
- 更有信心，而非制造焦虑 — 清晰减少不确定。先解释，再建议。
- 文化滋养，而非渲染神秘 — 传统是智慧，不是玄学。
